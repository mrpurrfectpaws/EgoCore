# EgoCore Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: March 2026**

### 1. Data Collection & Usage
EgoCore requests your Roblox User ID and Username via official Roblox OAuth 2.0 to:
* Verify account ownership.
* Sync your EgoGames group rank to your Discord roles.

### 2. Optional Birthday Feature
Users may optionally provide their birth date for the **Birthday Announcement** feature. This data is used solely for automated server announcements.

### 3. Data Retention & Deletion
* **Verification Data:** To ensure a hassle-free experience, your verification link is maintained if you leave the server. To permanently delete your verification data from our database, use the `/unverify` command.
* **Birthday Data:** Birthday information is automatically deleted from our database if you leave the Discord server. You may also manually delete it at any time using `/birthday remove`.

### 4. Security
We do not sell or share your data. All information is stored in a secure database and is only accessible by authorized EgoGames systems.
